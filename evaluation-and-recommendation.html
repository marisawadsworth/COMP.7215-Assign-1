Evaluation and Recommendation


Social Media (Twitter)

I recommend SPA because most of the pages don’t need to update, but only require content as most of the page as very similar to each other. E.g. everyone’s Twitter/Instagram profiles are the same layout just different content. The news feed is the same layout just different content etc. This will also improve the speed of the website.


Content streaming website (Spotify)

I recommend SPA because when you’re listening to Spotify, you don’t want the application to reload when you’re listening to music as it’ll re-load the song you’re listening to as well and turn it off.


Blogging/information website

I recommend off-the-shelf because when you have a blog, you spend a long time on a page, and one of the big reasons for having one is the SEO.


Business to business online sales website (business clients, charge accounts)

I recommend off-the-shelf because if the website has high traffic, lots of businesses need it to work reliably. SEO is a big part of marketing as it brings in customers. The server-side rendering will be much easier to fulfil requirements.


Micro jobbing site (Fiverr)

I recommend SPA because most of the pages don’t need to update, but only require content, this will improve the speed of the website. When a user wants to apply for a job it would take them to a form, this form could be a Google form which is a SPA website.


Event website for a local event

I recommend SPA because most of the pages don’t need to update, but only require content, this will improve the speed of the website.
